## AI-powered digital twins

AI-powered digital twins combine physics-based simulation with real-time operational data to create dynamic, evolving representations of products, machines, and entire production systems. By linking the virtual and physical worlds in a continuous feedback loop, they enable companies to design, validate, and optimize with far greater speed, confidence, and efficiency.

### Simulation-driven intelligence across the lifecycle

Integrate product and production models spanning mechanics, electronics, software, and automation to design, simulate, and virtually commission systems before physical build, reducing risk, cost, and time to market.

### Closed-loop optimization with real-world data

Capture data through sensors and IT/OT convergence, feed performance insights back into the twin, and use AI to analyze scenarios, predict behavior, and continuously improve throughput, quality, and scheduling.

### From predictive insight to autonomous decisions

Train AI models in the virtual environment, deploy executable twins close to the asset, and enable real-time analysis and decision support that increases resilience, efficiency, and sustainability across operations.
